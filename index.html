Privacy Policy for Aviary & Breeding Manager

Effective Date: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Aviary & Breeding Manager ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

2. Data Collection and Storage

Local Storage:
All data entered into the Aviary & Breeding Manager app (including cage details, bird records, health data, and financial information) is stored locally on your device using a local SQLite database. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal breeding data on any external servers.

Cloud Backup:
The app offers an optional Cloud Backup feature using Google Drive. If you choose to use this feature:

  • The app will request permission to access your Google Drive account.
  • The backup file is saved directly to your personal Google Drive.
  • We do not have access to your Google account credentials or the backup files stored in your Drive.

3. Third-Party Services

Currently, the app does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs (such as Google Analytics or AdMob) that track user behavior. If this changes in future updates, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

4. Permissions Required

To function properly, the app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage/Files: Required to save local database files and export Excel reports.
  • Internet/Network: Required only for the optional Google Drive backup feature.

5. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend using device-level security features (such as PIN, fingerprint, or facial recognition) to protect your information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since all data is stored locally, we do not process any personal data of minors.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
